Power BI

A dashboard report that provides a detailed and interactive view of essential metrics to help understand the performance and trends in the rental market.

AirBnB
Project

Here's an explanation of the different views included:

Average Price per Bedroom

Description: Displays the average nightly price of AirBnB listings categorized by the number of bedrooms. Purpose: Helps hosts and analysts understand pricing trends based on the number of bedrooms, aiding in competitive pricing strategies. Visualization: Typically shown as a bar chart or line graph.

Distinct Count of Bedroom Listings

Description: Shows the total number of unique AirBnB listings grouped by the number of bedrooms. Purpose: Provides insight into the availability and distribution of different types of accommodations. Visualization: Often displayed as a bar chart or pie chart.

Price per Zip Code

Description: Illustrates the variation in listing prices across different zip codes. Purpose: Helps identify high-demand areas and regions with higher rental rates. Visualization: Usually presented as a heat map or choropleth map.

Revenue for the Year

Description: Summarizes the total revenue generated from AirBnB rentals over the past year. Purpose: Allows stakeholders to track overall financial performance and identify revenue trends. Visualization: Commonly shown as a line chart or bar chart to highlight revenue changes over time.

Console Sales
Project

This report provides an overview of the sales performance of various brand consoles with a focus on different dashboard views, including global sales, Sony Entertainment global sales, a comparison of Sony console sales against principal competitors, and video game sales segmented by gender and platform.

Global Sales

This dashboard presents an aggregate view of console sales worldwide. It includes:

Total Units Sold: An overview of the total number of consoles sold globally.

Regional Breakdown: Sales data segmented by region (e.g., North America, Europe, Asia).

Yearly Trends: A line chart showing the sales trends over the years, indicating peaks and dips in sales.

Sony Entertainment Global Sales

This dashboard focuses exclusively on Sony Entertainment's console sales. It includes:

Units Sold: Total number of Sony consoles sold globally.

Sales by Model: Breakdown of sales by different Sony console models (e.g., PlayStation 3, PlayStation 4).

Market Share: Sony's market share compared to other brands in different regions.

Console Sales: Sony vs. Principal Competitors

This dashboard compares the sales performance of Sony consoles against their main competitors. It includes:

Head-to-Head Sales Comparison: Bar charts comparing the sales figures of Sony consoles with those of competitors like Microsoft (Xbox) and Nintendo.

Market Share Analysis: Pie charts showing the market share distribution among the top console manufacturers.

Sales by Gender: A breakdown of video game sales by gender, showing preferences and trends among male and female gamers.

Platform Preferences: Analysis of which consoles or platforms are more popular among different genders.

Top Selling Games: List of the top-selling games, segmented by platform and gender, to identify popular genres and titles.
